Verve Contactless

The Verve Contactless Card is an EMV card with dual interface technology issued by banks in partnership with Verve International to facilitate fast, secure and convenient payments at merchant locations and outlets.

Contactless payments simply require the cardholder to position their contactless Verve Card within a couple of inches of the POS terminal. The terminal automatically reads the linked account information stored on the chip embedded in the card and securely processes the payment transaction.

Benefits of Verve Contactless

  • Speed, convenience and security of payments at merchant locations.
  • No need to enter card details for transactions within the limits set by the Bank.
  • Accessible on all channels (POS, Web, ATM, Mobile).
  • Improved customer experience and satisfaction.

Verve Prepaid Card

Verve Prepaid Debit Card allows you to preload a customised card with any amount you desire. The pre-loaded funds are accessible at any POS or ATM locaton, and the card can be easily reloaded at any time.

One card, multiple possibilities

Payment Card:
Verve Prepaid card can serve as a Company Expense Card, Travel Card for company employees, Transport Card, Salary/Wage Card, Petty Cash Card, Fuel Card, and more.

Identity Card:
Upon request, Biometric functionalities on Verve Prepaid can be activated and this can serve as an identity card for staff members and workers.

Revenue Collection Token:
With Verve Prepaid, governmental bodies and agencies can accept payments from taxpayers without requiring their agents to handle cash. This offers full transparency, reduces fraud and offers flexibility and ease to taxpayers.
