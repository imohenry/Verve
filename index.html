Our Pan African Story

Leveraging a firm understanding of the African terrain, Verve emerged to take up the challlenge of
ensuring all segments of the African market are included and sufficiently connected to the possibilities
offered by card payments.

We introduced a more affordable card scheme option, secured with chip+PIN technology and compatible
with the customer's reality. Over a decade later, with a wide issuance and acceptance footprint within
and beyond the continent, Verve is still leading the way with payment solutions built for Africa, by Africans.

Here for Africa
